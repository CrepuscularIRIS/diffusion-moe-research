## WS1 — the index (TWO levels; Spearman-vs-frozen alone is insufficient)

Candidate scores at MPPI iteration k: `s_i^f = Σγ^h r̂(z_{i,h},a_{i,h}) + γ^H Q_f(z_{i,H})` (frozen)
and `s_i^a` (adapted critic), over the planner's ACTUAL candidate sets (logged for one cell in
`planner_query_gap`; other cells regenerated by forward passes on frozen ckpts — offline, no
closed-loop).

- **CI_rank (candidate-ranking consistency)** — elite-weighted pairwise agreement:
  `CI_rank = 1 − Σ_{i<j} ω_ij · 1[sgn(s_i^a−s_j^a) ≠ sgn(s_i^f−s_j^f)] / Σ ω_ij`,
  with ω_ij concentrated on pairs near the ELITE boundary (rank flips among low scorers don't move
  the proposal update).
- **CI_proposal (proposal-update consistency — closest to the confirmed failure locus):**
  `exp[−D_JS(w^a ‖ w^f)]` over the induced MPPI weights, or directly the induced-update distance
  `D_update = ‖Δμ_a−Δμ_f‖²_{Σ_f^{-1}} + c‖Δlogσ_a−Δlogσ_f‖²`. This level is the one plausibly able
  to explain DYNAMIC accumulation and the λ-sweep interior minimum; plain rank correlation vs the
  frozen endpoint is structurally near-monotone in λ and cannot.

**Retrodiction testbed (free, in the artifacts):** λ-sweep 11 arms incl. the interior minimum
(43→30→…→439, `lambda_threshold.json`) · decoupling arms (true-shift 50 / wrong-shift 29, both must
score LOW) · rank-norm arms (index must survive the normalization that didn't rescue) · severity grid
incl. the **cheetah-grav2× GRACEFUL cell** (cliff_ratio 0.936 — grading, not a binary) · reacher/
cartpole (predicted or explicitly out-of-scope).

## WS3 — the sufficiency experiment (the strongest possible result in the paper)

Train a **rank-distilled critic**: `L = L_shift-TD + λ_r·L_pairwise-rank + λ_b·L_Bellman-model`,
with the pairwise logistic rank loss `Σ log(1+exp[−y_ij(Q(z_i)−Q(z_j))])`, y_ij from the frozen /
model-consistent ranking on planner-encountered candidate sets. Target properties: absorbs post-shift
value information · preserves the learned model's candidate ranking · free numerical scale · may be
MORE accurate than frozen w.r.t. truth · substantially higher CI_proposal than the naive adapted
critic. **Does not collapse ⇒ first intervention that moves consistency while controlling accuracy —
the causal claim closes. Still collapses ⇒ rank-consistency-as-sufficient is abandoned and the
mechanism repositions.** Either outcome is decisive. (The affine-scale control from the multi-shift replication (ledger row W2, 2026-07-13) was
most plausibly a scale control on the COLLAPSING critic — verify WHICH critic was transformed before
counting it as evidence either way; the rank-distilled construction is the load-bearing one.)

## WS4 — the method: Consistency-Guarded MPPI (proposal-trust-region value injection)

Per iteration k, blend scores `s_i(β) = (1−β)s_i^f + β s_i^a` and pick
`β_k* = max{β ∈ B}` subject to
`D_KL(q_{k+1}^β ‖ q_{k+1}^0) ≤ δ` (the induced NEXT proposal stays in a trust region of the
frozen-critic update) and `τ_w(s(β), s^f) ≥ κ` (weighted Kendall τ_w, elite-emphasized — the rank-agreement floor).
**β is NOT a fixed hyperparameter — it adapts per candidate set per iteration.** Structurally distinct
from every falsified arm: not a fixed 0.5 blend · not argmax-at-selection-only · not more sampling
budget · not critic fine-tuning; it constrains exactly the confirmed failure locus (proposal
evolution). Expected signature: graceful cells admit large β; cliff cells drive β down automatically.
**Degeneracy check (nontriviality): if β* ≈ 0 everywhere, the method has degenerated into an
abstention detector and may NOT be claimed as a solution.**

## Pre-registered Go/No-Go gates ("all runs finished" is NO LONGER a completion criterion)

- **CONSTRUCT gate** — the index has an explicit formula, estimator, aggregation rule (across
  states/iterations), and threshold.
- **PREDICTION gate** — held-out collapse vs graceful cells separated WITHOUT target-cell returns;
  must specifically account for cheetah-grav2× graceful AND the λ interior minimum.
- **INTERVENTION gate** — consistency-guarded MPPI vs {frozen, full-MC, fixed blend, mitigation B,
  2×-budget} under IDENTICAL sampling, budget, and final-selection rules (the §7.1 factorial
  discipline, inherited).
- **NONTRIVIALITY gate** — the method demonstrably USES the adapted critic somewhere (β>0 with gains
  over frozen, or a clear safety–adaptation Pareto improvement); never safety-by-always-frozen.

Paper-level success: aggregate return significantly beats all single-component baselines ·
catastrophic degradation eliminated in cliff cells · graceful cells not materially harmed · ≥ several
preregistered cells beat frozen with CI>0 · β*/index predicts held-out collapse severity.
